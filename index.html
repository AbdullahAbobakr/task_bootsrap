The Siwa Oasis is an urban oasis in Egypt. It is situated between the Qattara and the Great Sand Sea in the Western Desert, 50 kilometres east of the Egypt-Libya border and 560 kilometres from the Egyptian capital city of Cairo.the ruins of which are a popular tourist attraction.

Popular Destiantions In Egypt

The Giza Pyramids in Egypt is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. In Egypt there are a large number of pyramids. But there are three great pyramids, namely the pyramid of Khufu, the pyramid of Khafre, and the pyramid of Menkaure. Many tourists from all over the world come to see the pyramids. The pyramids show the civilization and the progress that the ancient Egyptians reached.

Luxor is a city built on the remains of the ancient ancient city of Thebes. Thebes was the capital of Egypt during the Middle and New Kingdom periods, i.e. over the period from about -2000 to -1000 BC. It was then the most revered city in Egypt, and included temples and a necropolis.

The city of Alexandria is the second capital of Egypt, after Cairo, and Alexandria remained the first capital of Egypt from its inception until the Islamic conquest of Egypt came, and this was at the hands of Amr Ibn Al-Aas, who took his capital, the city of Fustat.
